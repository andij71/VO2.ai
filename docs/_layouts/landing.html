How it works

01

Connect Strava

Authorize once via OAuth. Your recent activities are pulled into the app so the coach can see what you actually ran this week.

02

Bring your own AI key

Paste your OpenRouter API key. Every chat goes straight from your phone to the model you picked. We never see it.

03

Train with a coach

Ask for a plan. Discuss yesterday's tempo run. Adjust on the fly when life gets in the way. Like texting a running friend who actually reads your data.

Privacy by design

0 servers.

The full count of VO2.ai infrastructure.

No accounts.
No analytics.
No backend.

There is no VO2.ai server. The app talks directly to Strava, directly to OpenRouter, and stores everything else locally on your device. Your API key lives in the iOS Keychain. Your chat history, plans, and profile live in a local database that you can wipe with one tap in Settings → Delete Account & Data. Because the entire source code is public, you can verify every network call and every piece of stored data yourself.
