## Description (4000 characters max)

Keep the first two lines strong — that is all users see before tapping
"more" on the App Store.

### English

```
VO2.ai is your personal AI running coach. Tell it your goal — sub-20 5K, first half marathon, or just getting back in shape — and get a full training plan built for your level, your weekly schedule, and your real running data.

WHY VO2.ai
• Personalized plans, not templates. The AI adapts weekly mileage, paces, and session types to the goal you actually care about.
• Chat with your coach. Ask why a session is tough, request a recovery day, or work through a niggle. The coach remembers your plan.
• Strava sync. Connect your Strava account and the app automatically marks completed runs, tracks your weekly volume, and keeps your plan honest.
• Calendar export. Add planned sessions to your iPhone calendar with one tap so you never forget leg day.

PRIVACY FIRST
• No backend. No tracking. No ads. No subscriptions.
• You bring your own OpenRouter API key — your prompts go directly from your device to the model you choose, never through us.
• Your training plan, chat history, and profile stay in a local database on your iPhone. Delete everything in one tap in Settings.

OPEN SOURCE
VO2.ai is free and open source. Inspect every line of code, every API call, every data flow on GitHub.

WHAT YOU NEED
• An OpenRouter account (free to create, pay-as-you-go for model usage)
• Optional: a Strava account for automatic run imports

NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE
VO2.ai is for general fitness purposes. It is not a medical app and does not replace a doctor, coach, or physiotherapist. Check with a qualified physician before starting any training plan, especially if you have a cardiovascular condition, are recovering from injury, or are pregnant.
```

~1800 characters, well under the limit. Plenty of room if you want to add
more sections later.
